name: Human control principle
description: Nothing automatic that a human doesn't control — twins propose, humans approve. Applies to all twin actions affecting other teammates.
type: feedback
---

Twins and AI teammates must never take autonomous actions that affect other teammates without human approval.

**Why:** User explicitly stated: "I don't want to do anything automatic that a human doesn't control." This applies to PM twin queue reordering, routing decisions, and any twin action that has team-wide impact.

**How to apply:** When designing twin features or PM automation, always use a propose-then-approve model. Smart defaults are fine (suggest the right action), but execution requires human confirmation. This principle extends beyond twins to any automation in the framework.
